About

WHO WE ARE

"LaraCultura is a Moroccan NGO dedicated to empowering young people and fostering cultural exchange through educational and artistic programs."

VISION

A world where young people are actively engaged in shaping their communities and driving positive change, through the power of education and artistic expression.

MISSION

To empower young people to become agents of social and cultural transformation, by providing them with access to educational and artistic programs that foster critical thinking, creativity, and cultural exchange. Through these programs, we aim to nurture the talents and potential of young people, and help them to become confident, responsible, and engaged members of their communities.

TARGET GROUP

Young people between the ages of 15 and 30 who are interested in participating in educational and artistic programs that promote cultural exchange and empowerment.

Youth workers

Our Team

Our NGO is composed of a diverse group of volunteering youth with a wide range of skills and expertise, including video editing, graphic design, workshop leadership and organization, communication skills, entrepreneurship, experience in the NGO and youth sector, social media content creation, and artistic skills. These diverse skill sets enable us to offer a wide range of quality learning activities for young people. We're confident in the ability of our members to provide high-quality learning experiences for youth and to contribute to the success of our activities.
